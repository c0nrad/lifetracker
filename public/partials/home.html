LifeTracker

LifeTracker is a system that helps you stay happy through journaling.

How does it work?

Create Account

Sign in using your gmail account. LifeTracker only records your name and email address, all other information is kept confidential. Once you've signed in you are set. No need to remember extra usernames or passwords!

Write what you are grateful for

Positive psychology shows that simply writing about things that make you grateful can make you a happier person! So everyday we challenge you to write three things that you are grateful. We also ask you to record if you worked out, meditated, and did a random act of kindess.

Create reminders

Creating a happier world for yourself isn't a sprint, it's a marathon, something that needs to be worked on everyday. That's why LifeTracker also encoroprates reminders, to help you accomplish your goals everyday.

Be Happy

The last and most important step. Enjoy your happiness! Life is short, go make the best of it. If this site helped, tell a friend!
